# App Store Release Guide

This guide will walk you through submitting the Hostly app to the Apple App Store. Follow each step carefully, and don't worryâ€”you can save your progress at any time.

---

## Step 1: Sign In to App Store Connect

1. Go to https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/apps
2. Sign in with your Apple Developer account credentials
3. Once logged in, find and click on the **Hostly** app from your list of apps

![App Store Connect Apps List](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5566b04f-0315-4051-bf98-208d202ac754)

---

## Step 2: Fill Out App Information

Before submitting your app for review, you need to complete the general app information.

1. In the left sidebar, click **App Information** (under the "General" section)
2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Name**: The app name that will appear on the App Store
   - **Subtitle**: A brief tagline (optional but recommended)
   - **Primary Category**: Choose the category that best fits your app
   - **Secondary Category**: Optional, if your app fits multiple categories
   - **Content Rights**: Indicate if your app contains third-party content
   - **Age Rating**: Complete the questionnaire to determine the appropriate age rating
3. Click **Save** in the top-right corner

![App Information Page](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f507590d-12af-4727-a8dc-01de4047df28)

> **Note**: You can skip optional sections if they don't apply to your app.

---

## Step 3: Set Up App Privacy

Apple requires you to disclose what data your app collects and how it's used.

1. In the left sidebar, click **App Privacy** (under the "Trust & Safety" section)
2. Enter your **Privacy Policy URL** (this should be a web page explaining your privacy practices)
3. Click **Get Started** to fill out the data collection questionnaire
4. Answer all questions honestly based on what data your app collects (examples: location, contact info, usage data, etc.)
5. Click **Publish** to save your privacy information

![App Privacy Section](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b1a57c31-9b30-49bb-b112-165777b88cf4)

> **Important**: Be accurate with your privacy disclosuresâ€”incorrect information can cause rejection.

---

## Step 4: Configure Pricing & Availability

Decide how much your app will cost and where it will be available.

1. In the left sidebar, click **Pricing & Availability** (under the "Monetization" section)
2. Click **Add Pricing**
3. In the price field, select **$0.00** to make your app free to download
4. Click **Set Up Availability**
5. Choose one of the following:
   - **All Countries or Regions**: Makes your app available worldwide
   - **Specific Countries or Regions**: Select only the countries where you want your app available
6. Click **Save**

![Pricing & Availability Settings](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/52b177d9-1108-4989-a5bd-db33fb9c07af)

---

## Step 5: Prepare Your Submission

Now you'll add screenshots, descriptions, and select which app version to release.

1. In the left sidebar, click **Prepare for Submission** (or the version number, e.g., "1.0")
  ![Prepare for Submission Overview](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/afcd2d83-fc89-45e3-892f-24ee341eec7b)  
2. Complete each section:

### Screenshots
- Upload screenshots for both **iPhone** and **iPad**
- Follow Apple's screenshot specifications: https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/reference/screenshot-specifications
- You'll need screenshots in different sizes for various device types

### App Description
- **Promotional Text**: A short description that appears at the top (can be updated anytime)
- **Description**: Full description of your app's features and functionality
- **Keywords**: Comma-separated keywords to help users find your app (max 100 characters)
- **Support URL**: Where users can get help (example: https://rallythelocals.com/contact-us)
- **Marketing URL**: Optional promotional website

### Build Selection
- Click **Add Build** (or the "+" icon next to Build)
- Select the app version you want to release from the list
- This is the actual app file that your development team uploaded

![Build Selection](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/190b4d54-3f3f-40c9-a782-1dae6a51f72c)

### App Review Information
- Provide a **test account** (username and password) so Apple reviewers can test your app
- Fill in **contact information** in case Apple needs to reach you during review

### Version Release
Choose how you want to release your app after approval:
- **Automatically release this version**: App goes live immediately after approval
- **Manually release this version**: You control when to make the app available after approval

3. Click **Save** at the top of the page
![Version Release Options](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c758692c-5278-4fef-8684-59b1812d1c3d)
> **Tip**: You can ignore the "App Clip" and "iMessage App" sections unless you specifically built features for these.

---

## Step 6: Submit for Review

You're ready to submit your app to Apple for review!

1. Go back to **Prepare for Submission** (or your version page)
2. Review all your information to make sure everything is complete
3. Click **Add for Review** button at the top

![Add for Review Button](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b50f9e2e-4dce-44b0-baae-5255462c9c53)

4. A "Draft Submission" dialog will appearâ€”review the details
5. Click **Submit for Review**
6. If everything is correct, you'll see the submission page with the status **"Waiting for Review"**

![Waiting for Review Status](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e07b1085-17bd-4b15-b1c0-e6479879cc36)

---

## What Happens Next?

After submission, your app will go through these stages:

1. **Waiting for Review**: Your app is in the queue (can take 1-3 days)
2. **In Review**: Apple is actively reviewing your app (usually 24-48 hours)
3. **Pending Developer Release**: Approved! If you chose manual release, you can now publish it
4. **Ready for Sale**: Your app is live on the App Store! ðŸŽ‰
